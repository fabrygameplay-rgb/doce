Meet Paige Three!

This fan page is dedicated to showcasing every runway look of Paige Three, a fan favourite from RuPaul's Drag Race UK Series 7. Paige Three is currently 29 years old and is Sasha Colby's drag daughter. Her real name is Louis Clarke-Clare, and she uses she/her pronouns in drag and he/him out of drag. Paige Three has a background in theatrical performance, which allows her to execute impressive front and mid splits with jumps. During RPDR UK Series 7, she placed 7th overall. She was eliminated in episode 7 by the eventual series winner, Bones, after facing her in the Snatch Game lip-sync to "Tainted Love" by Soft Cell. Paige Three is known for her bold use of bodysuits, a style choice that was highlighted by Michelle Visage during the Talent Show, where Paige placed low and narrowly avoided a lip-sync against Bones or Chai Tea Grande. Despite this, she achieved a standout moment by winning The Rusical alongside Elle Vosque, earning a double win for that week and showcasing her theatrical and performance skills. On this website, you will find extra tea, including her most powerful and dramatic moments, her entrance line, and her final words. You'll also find a chronological categorization of her runway looks, a track record explaining her performance, and other juicy details extracted from her Wiki to make this page even more entertaining and interesting.
